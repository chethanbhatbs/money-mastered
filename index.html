About

The Design

Broadsheet editorial style. Fraunces (display), Spectral (body), Archivo (small-caps furniture) — ink on warm paper, with a single gold accent. No drop-shadows, no rounded "cards", no generic SaaS look — the visual language of a serious printed broadsheet, transposed to screen and print.

Real charts. Every market chart in Book 5 is built from real OHLC data — S&P 500, AAPL, NFLX, META, TSLA, Nifty 50, Reliance — rendered through mplfinance against the data. Every figure is independently verifiable; the back of Book 5 lists every chart with instrument and date range.

Real math. Every formula is followed by a worked numeric calculation. Compound growth, real return (Fisher), expense-ratio drag, the 1% position-sizing rule, Bollinger Bands, ATR, RSI, MACD, Ichimoku — all computed by hand and shown step by step.

Real depth. Book 5 alone is 42 chapters in 10 parts, with case studies of the 1929, 1987, 2000, 2008, 2020 and 2022 episodes on actual price data, a 60+ exercise workbook with full answer keys, and a Python companion that ships code for every indicator.
